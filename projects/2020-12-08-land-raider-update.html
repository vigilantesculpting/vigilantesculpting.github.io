Land Raider upgrades

A bit of rework of my original papercraft Land Raider, my first project (with some comparison photos).


I added some extra detail, such as the space marine gunner, and gave it a much better looking paint job with some weathering techniques I picked up over the past years.

You may recognize some of the greeblie detail (including the gunner!) are casts from other projects.
