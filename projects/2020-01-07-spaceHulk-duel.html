Spacehulk Duel!


> *It only took a millisecond.*
>
> *"What's that noise?"*
> *"Don't worry, it's probably just a rat, the place is crawling with
> them"*
>
> *The genestealer burst from the vent above, ripping the marine's
> helmet off in one sweeping motion. Instinctively he caught the lashing
> front claw in his powerfist, and tried to get a couple of burst off
> with his bolter, but the beast was too strong and pushed the weapon
> out of the way, the blasts harmlesly impacting the pipe behind.*
>
> *Locked in a mortal embrace, they stumbled backwards into the gaping
> cavern below…*

This was a fun little project, depicting an epic duel between a genestealer
and a Deathwing Terminator. Lots of weathering, and I think the action
is pretty dynamic.
