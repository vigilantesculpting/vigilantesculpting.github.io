Sabre Strike

Papercraft + mixed media scratchbuilt Sabre Strike Tank.

I started from a template by Eli Patoroch, and added my own mix of resin cast tracks from a master mold, various bits and pieces from previous molds, electrical wire, drinking straws etc.Very fun.
