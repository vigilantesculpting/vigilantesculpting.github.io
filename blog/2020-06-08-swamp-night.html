Swamp night

Published on 08/06/2020 @ 12:15:00 by vigilante sculpting

I added a first lighting pass using white ink over black primer, and then glazed in some color with the airbrush, trying to see if I can make the scene look like what I have in my head.
So far so good. I'm planning to paint the dread as a Black Templar unit, hunting for orks on a swamp world.
