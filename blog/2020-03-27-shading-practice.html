Shading practice

Published on 27/03/2020 @ 18:32:00 by vigilante sculpting

Practicing shading of skin, using pencil, charcoal + brushpen.
I really suck at this, so to practice I traced a photo reference onto a piece of paper, and tried to shade it as best I could.
Scanned and post in GIMP, to darken some areas and improve the contrast.
