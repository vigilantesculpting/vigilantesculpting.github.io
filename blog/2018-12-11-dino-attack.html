Dino attack!

Published on 11/12/2018 @ 10:37:00 by vigilante sculpting

So my 6yo daughter also wanted to play with clay. I asked her what she wanted to make and she said a T-rex. I googled some reference pictures for her, made her an armature with wire and foil, and then gave her a ball of sculpey.

I did give her a hand with the shape of the upper and lower jaws, and showed her how to make the eyes, but the rest is her work.

The first piece of sculpey I gave her had some fimo mixed in, that's why it is darker.
