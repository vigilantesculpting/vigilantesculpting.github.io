Huntress

Published on 18/10/2019 @ 11:58:00 by vigilante sculpting

Saint Toad had recently been posting a couple of beautiful bases on coolminiornot. One especially caught my eye, and after I mentioned how it just begs for a mini to be put on it, he graciously sent it to me.

I started sculpting a female huntress from mostly beesputty, ready to release a shot from her bow.
Starting to love the beesputty...
