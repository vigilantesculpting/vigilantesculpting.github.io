Main gun

Published on 16/04/2021 @17:13:00 by vigilante sculpting

Starting work on the main gun:

Lots of ribbing for extra… plasma?

I made the ribs from sculpey, which I rolled out with a large threaded rod / bolt, and shaped over an armature.This is different from what I've done in the past, where I used wire wrapped around a shape, but in this case the ribs have a squared off shape to them plus I wanted the ribs to be deep in order to pick up paint better.

This is the shell of the gun. Spot the corn flakes box :)

and posed with the ribbing:

Here is the greeblie that goes on top. This was fun and quick to make :)
