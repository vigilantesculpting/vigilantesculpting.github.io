Hunter Killer

Published on 2020/06/11 by vigilante sculpting

  • Blog Post
  • #project:hunterkiller
  • #dreadnought
  • #diorama
  • #swamp
  • #35mm

  • #base
  • #lighting
  • #osl

I painted the shoulders a bone white then washed with Payne's gray ink, to make it colder and look like the dread is walking around the swamp in the dark.The armor has blue-grey edge highlighting for the same reason.The piece of red cloth (left behind by the target that the dread is hunting) should look semi-see-through, as if the light is hitting it hard enough.

Of course I still need to add metallics, insignia and some more weathering. Plus the actual water of the swamp. So this is far from done.
