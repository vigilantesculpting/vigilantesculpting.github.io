Fantasy style building

Published on 2021/03/23 by vigilante sculpting

  • Blog Post
  • scratchbuilt
  • fantasy
  • building

My daughter got very inspired after seeing some of Dented Armor's (https://www.instagram.com/dentedarmor/) work on instagram, where he builds these fantasticfantasy style buildings, so I spent some time on Sunday working with my kids to build a forest cottage for them.Of course, they want to play with their Lego people inside the building, so I opted for leaving the back of the cottage exposed.It is not yet complete: I still need to add some bricks around the chimney for example, but they are super amped about it and now want an entire city lol.
