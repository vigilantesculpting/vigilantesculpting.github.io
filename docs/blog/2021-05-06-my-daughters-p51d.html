My daughter's P-51D

Published on 06/05/2021 @12:07:00 by vigilante sculpting

Last but not least, my daughter really wanted a model aircraft to build for her 9th birthday.So I swung by our local friendly hobby shoppe, and got her a Revell 1/48th scale P-51D Mustang.She is super excited about it, here she is painting the cockpit after assembly.

Last night after she went to bed I did a little extra freehand detailing on the instrument panel. I couln't help myself lol
