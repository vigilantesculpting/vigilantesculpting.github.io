Oiled up

Published on 20/11/2019 @10:22:00 by vigilante sculpting

Playing with oils on the tech priest. This is the first time I'm using oils for the majority of a piece, instead of acrylics.

I am loving it! The cloak is super easy to blend, because the oil stays malleable for so long. The washes flow into features like magic. The colors are super saturated.
