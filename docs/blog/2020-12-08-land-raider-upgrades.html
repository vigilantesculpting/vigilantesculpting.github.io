Land Raider upgrades

Published on 08/12/2020 @11:49:00 by vigilante sculpting

I'm calling the Land Raider rework project done. It has been fun, revisiting my first papercraft tank and adding some refinement to it.In addition to the physical elements, I've added a lot of the weathering techniques I've picked up so far, so lots of chipping and oil streaking/staining, plus the barrel heat staining and the mud.And the OSL on the headlights looks much better than before, even if it isn't perfect yet.

I will leave it here though, time to move on, and hopefully in a couple of years I'll be able to look back and see how much I've improved :)
