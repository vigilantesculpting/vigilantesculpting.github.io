Delta Jet, finished!

Published on 21/12/2018 @22:54:00 by vigilante sculpting

I've finished my sculpt of Delta Jet. She's my third sculpt at 35mm.

Overall I'm happy. I think I've got her pose and general body shape just right. She is obviously female, but not over-proportioned.

The open hand could do with some work, but again this is my first attempt, and I don't think it came out too bad. What I'm not very happy with is her face and hair. These are very tricky though, so I just have to practice more.

She is over at puttyandpaint, as well as ArtStation.
And as always, please go and vote for her at coolminiornot.
