Dredd - Painting

Published on 05/10/2021 @11:36:10 by vigilante sculpting

Working on all the textures: the reference image has a ton of different textures; it was fun sculpting them, and the painting is turning out to be super enjoyable too.

The skin got a magenta oil wash, this really adds some life!

Aiming for some NMM on the larger metallic surfaces, I want to really try and control where the highlights and shadows go before applying the bloody splatters.

I added a black oil wash to the base, and this makes the lights look grimy and for some reason makes the OSL pop even more, something I didn't expect #happyaccidents.
