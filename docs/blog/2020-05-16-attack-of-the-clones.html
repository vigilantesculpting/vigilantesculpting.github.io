Attack of the clones

Published on 2020/05/16 by vigilante sculpting

  • Blog Post
  • project:powerarmor
  • sculpting
  • greenstuff
  • sculpey
  • 35mm
  • diy
  • wip
  • resincasting

I've been cloning the power armor dude, and started hacking the bodies apart and reattaching and reposing the limbs with some greenstuff.The goal is to get a squad of these guys together, each with different weapons.In typical heavy power armor (ala WH40k Terminators), there will be a squad leader, three regulars and a heavy weapons guy.Of course, this is still rough WIP, so no weapons yet and some of the casts have heaps of bubbles and holes in them that need fixing.
