Vanilla Joe, done!

Published on 05/02/2021 @23:58:00 by vigilante sculpting

After a lot of sanding, priming, re-sanding and re-priming, I'm now finished with the sculpting of Vanilla Joe Pineapples. He is ready to be covered in a thick blanket of silicon so I can make some casts of the pieces.Here are some pictures of him. There are a couple of last-minute sanding spots where I didn't bother re-priming, only because he will serve as that master model for the mold.

From one of the cast pieces I will make Chocolate Joe Pineapples, by sculpting on the leather corset, gloves and adding the fur cloak.
