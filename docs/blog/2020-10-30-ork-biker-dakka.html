Ork biker dakka

Published on 30/10/2020 @15:50:00 by vigilante sculpting

Did some more work on ork biker dude: I realised he had a bit of a dumb look on his face, and while he is an ork, I'd like him to look more... menacing? lol.

So I sculpted a fatter lower lip and some stark eyebrows, then gave him some color and a little familiar-looking logo. Still wip...The rocketbike got some checkers, because dakka.
