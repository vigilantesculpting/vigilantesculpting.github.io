Wardog

Published on 11/12/2018 @10:59:00 by vigilante sculpting

Here is a roughly 50mm sculpt I made a looong time ago in a country far away, when I was a young pup:

It is based on the Imperial Rottweilers from the Khronicles of Khaos (2000AD), designed by one of my favourite artists Kev Walker

Copyright 2000AD

I thought it is time to redo it in 35mm. Here is my wip

I figure he has to stand a bit more crouched, and that left arm has to be tucked a bit, but otherwise a good start.
