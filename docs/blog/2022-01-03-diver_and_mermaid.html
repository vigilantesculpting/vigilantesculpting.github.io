Diver and mermaid

Published on 03/01/2022 @14:02:06 by vigilante sculpting

I've been making some progress on an idea that I have had kicking around for quite a while.

The idea is an underwater diorama, where a diver and mermaid meets.Here is the diver:

and here is the mermaid:

Here is a mockup of the diorama, you can see the underwater mine they are swimming around,and the bubbles from the diver. The positions are not final, they need some tweaking, and I think I went overboard (no pun intended) with the bubbles, but the basic shapes are there.
