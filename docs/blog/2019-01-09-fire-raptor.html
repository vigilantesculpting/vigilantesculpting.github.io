Fire Raptor

Published on 2019/01/09 by vigilante sculpting

  • Blog Post
  • #scratchbuilt
  • #project:fireraptor
  • #mixed
  • #diy
  • #wip
  • #wh40k

I'm putting together a scratchbuilt Fire Raptor gunship, based on a template which I downloaded from papermodelers.com. It is mostly papercraft, but I plan using a bit of mixed media.

It is an interesting build. I plan to make some modifications to it, the first major one being that I want to model the interior of the craft, with a hinged rear-entry ramp and the hinged hatch on top.

I've started the build log at papermodelers.com.
