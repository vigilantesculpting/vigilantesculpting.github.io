Stormblade done!

Published on 19/08/2021 @15:01:23 by vigilante sculpting

Finishing up the project, it has been a great ride!

Using the airbrush, I deepened the blue on the hull, and gave it a light gray drybrush. Then added the muzzle burn on the lasguns, gave everything another once over with black and brown oil paint, and finishedup with a satin varnish, then finally redusted with some pigment.
