Swamp hunter

Published on 06/06/2020 @12:15:00 by vigilante sculpting

These last few weeks, and especially the last few days, have been pretty draining, I've found it hard to do more than just sit around. I hope everyone is doing ok.

I've haven't progressed much on the power armor guys, but instead have picked up the original scratch built papercraft dreadnought that I built previously, and started making a base for it.This one will be a swamp:

I made a little search light for him, and built a plasma weapon. Lots of potential for some lighting effects here, I think.

Thanks for looking and stay safe.
