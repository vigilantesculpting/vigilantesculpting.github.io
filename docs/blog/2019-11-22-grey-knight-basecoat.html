Grey Knight Basecoat

Published on 22/11/2019 @09:30:00 by vigilante sculpting

Primed the Grey Knight Terminator in black, then basecoated in metallic platinum, followed by a zenithal coat from above with blue ink and from below with burnt umber + yellow.
The yellow makes for too much green, so I might follow it up with either red or sepia, just to get a more orange-ish tone on the lower parts.
