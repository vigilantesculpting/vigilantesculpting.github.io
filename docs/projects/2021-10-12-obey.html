OBEY

Published on 12/10/2021 @10:58:41 by vigilante sculpting

This is a little diorama of Judge Dredd, based on the amazing work of Simon Bisley.

I remember seeing a poster of the SB piece on a friend's dorm door, and being absolutely blown away by the grittiness, the colors, the poses… consider this is a little tribute & fanart.
