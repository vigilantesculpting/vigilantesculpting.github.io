Ultranauts, assemble!

Published on 14/08/2020 @15:15:00 by vigilante sculpting

These are some of the first miniatures that I sculpted.

I refer to them as the Ultranauts, with the brave but clumsy space captain Ace Bolt, his very competent and beautiful co-pilot Delta Jet, and their calm and cool alien companion Octon.

Mostly sculpey and a bit of Fimo, all about 35mm tall.
