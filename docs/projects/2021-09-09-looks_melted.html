"Somebody must have bagged one of Ripley's bad guys"

I love Aliens, and this is my tribute to H.R. Giger, James Cameron,and the iconic cast, special effects crews and everyone that brought this movie to us.

The project start in August 2020, and I finished it in September 2021. Construction went fairly quickly, including the sculpted marine and xenomorph. But painting took a lot longer.I had in my head a vision of the contrasts of warm/cool, light and dark that I wanted to show, but the colors I had on there just never came together until now. Very happy with the end result.

Enjoy!
