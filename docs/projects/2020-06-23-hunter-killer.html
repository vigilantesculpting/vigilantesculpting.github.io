Scratchbuilt papercraft dreadnought, painted up in the Black Templars livery and posed on a swampy base made of of natural material and epoxy resin.

I had a good play with OSL on this one, trying to imitate the way the strong searchlight would light up both the swamp & tree stump, and thetorn cloth of the ork that is trying to get away.
