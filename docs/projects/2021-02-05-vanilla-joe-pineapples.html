Vanilla Joe Pineapples

Published on 05/02/2021 @23:58:00 by vigilante sculpting

This is a sculpt I did of Joe Pineapples, the ultra cool sniper from 2000AD's ABC Warriors.

Based on the awesome Prog 787 (alternate link) cover by Kev Walker, with Joe having had his brain circuitry "reorganized" by Khaos, he is posing with his rifle, dressed in a very risque outfit.

This is the vanilla Joe version, without the outfit. I still plan to sculpt the outfit, in what I refer to as Chocolate Joe.
